Degradation and Failure Models

A deterministic core driven by stochastic inputs. 
INPUTS
Drivers
Temperature
Humidity
Operating load
Cycles since maintenance

→
CORE
Degradation rate
$\lambda_i = \lambda_{0,i} \cdot \alpha_i \cdot f_{\text{ext}} \cdot f_{\text{int}} \cdot f_{\text{cross}}$
$H_i(t+1) = H_i(t) - \lambda_i$
→
STATE
Health Index
$H_i \in [0, 1]$
Per component, per step (day).
Persisted to historian.
→
OUTPUT
Failure
End-of-life threshold
or random shock event
Type I · Wear-out
End of useful life.
Health index drops below a critical threshold. Natural component wear, fully predicted by the model.
Type II · Operational shock
Negligence event.
Probability 1 in 300 prints. A random component fails regardless of its current state — captures the unmodelable.
